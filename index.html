Celebrating 35 years of the World Wide Web

The World Wide Web turns 35 years old on March 12.The evolution of the web has been tremendous over the last few years, right from web1.0 to web3.0. we’ve compiled a timeline of the most important milestones in the web’s history.

March 1989

Tim Berners-Lee, a British scientist, invented the World Wide Web (WWW)

WEB 1.0 (1989)

Web 1.0 was first coined by Tim Berners-Lee in 1989. It is treated as the first generation of World Wide Web

Number Of Internet Users:2.6 Million

Number Of internet users reached 2.6 Million

1994 First Online Banner AD

It simply read: “Have you ever clicked your mouse right HERE?

AUGUST 1995 MICROSOFT EXPLORER

The first version of Internet Explorer,made its debut on August 17, 1995.

SEPTEMBER 1998 GOOGLE!

Google's focus on technology and indexing gives the impression that it is a gateway to the whole web.

OCTOBER 28 2003 FACEBOOK

Facebook is a social networking service originally launched as FaceMash on October 28, 2003.

2004 WEB 2.0

The concept of Web 2.0 was coined by Dale Dougherty,web pioneer and O'Reilly VP in 2004.

JULY 2006 TWITTER

The first Twitter prototype, developed by Dorsey and contractor Florian Weber, was used as an internal service for Odeo employees.

2006 WEB 3.0

Web 3.0 added machine readability features to the web documents.Web 3.0 is also known as semantic web.

APRIL 2012 INSTAGRAM

Instagram is a photo and video-sharing social media application that was launched in 2010 by Kevin Systrom.

2014 BLOCHAIN IN WEB

The blockchain-based social protocol of Follow intends to offer complete control of the social identities and data of users to them.
